About Me

Musthak Kodinhi, born on 17 May 1976 in Kodinhi, Malappuram, Kerala, is an Indian journalist, writer, cartoonist, filmmaker, and illustrator. He is widely recognized for his contributions to Malayalam journalism and docufiction filmmaking.

With over 20 years of experience in media and storytelling, Musthak specializes in investigative journalism, developmental reporting, and visual storytelling. His work spans multiple disciplines, each bringing unique perspectives to contemporary issues.

Career Overview

📰

Journalism

Musthak began his career in print media, serving as a reporter for leading Malayalam dailies including 'Chandrika' and 'Suprabhaatham'. He also served as the Executive Editor of the 'Pravasa Dhara' monthly.

As a prolific writer, he has contributed numerous articles to various periodicals and has authored over 17 specialized series focusing on diverse social and agricultural topics.

🎨

Art & Illustration

Beyond his extensive writing career, Musthak is an accomplished illustrator and cartoonist whose visual commentary resonates across print media.

His illustrations and cartoons have appeared in several periodicals, and he notably served as the back-page cartoonist for 'Aaramam Magazine' for over a year.

🎬

Filmmaking

Musthak has dynamically ventured into cinema and documentary filmmaking, specializing heavily in "docufiction"—a masterful blend of documentary realism and narrative fictional elements.

Notable Projects:

  • 'TMnte Bharatheeyar' (Docufiction) – Screenplay, Dialogues, & Direction
  • 'Colour Chalk' (Docufiction) – Director
  • 'Sathyappalli' (Documentary) – Director
